THE LIFE OF A D1 ATHLETE

A Short Film

This mockumentary style short film was inspired by basketball players like Kobe Bryant, showcasing the insane work ethic and hard work that goes into being a professional sports player. I also used the montages as a way to showcase scenic spots around campus, as well as our world class athletics facilities. Huge thanks to my friend, Ingus Dimisevskis, for sacrificing his saturday morning to film.
